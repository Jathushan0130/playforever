Play Forever is a non-profit organization providing free and accessible recreation, education & health services to youth and seniors.

Our dream is to see youth and seniors in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) access health services and recreation opportunities, learn job-ready skills, and build lasting community.

The journey of Play Forever began with a simple yet powerful idea: to bring joy and opportunity to the youth of underserved areas in Toronto. It all started when a group of passionate individuals, deeply moved by the disparities in access to recreational activities, came together with a shared vision. Fueled by their commitment to making a difference, they set out to create a space where every child could thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances. Through hard work, determination, and the unwavering support of the community, what began as a humble initiative quickly blossomed into a full-fledged nonprofit organization. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, expanding our reach and impact, but our core mission remains unchanged: to provide young people with the tools and opportunities they need to succeed on and off the field. Today, as we reflect on our journey, we are filled with gratitude for the countless individuals who have joined us along the way, each one playing a vital role in shaping the story of Play Forever and the lives of the children we serve.
